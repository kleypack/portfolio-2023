My values

Though I’m not always in a position to make decisions, I will always push to do the right thing.

Focus on people

And their problems. Utilise prototyping and iteration to help people get stuff done.

Empower and delight

Make things simple, but not soulless. Build things that are accessible, understandable, and joyful.

Respect and protect

Advocate for ethical design and the need to balance business needs with moral and socially-conscious decisions. Fight dark patterns and honour privacy.
